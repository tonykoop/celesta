# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, bar lengths, resonator sizes, hammer weights, action ratios, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a finished celesta prototype: a refined compact keyboard cabinet with a partially open top revealing graduated polished steel bars suspended above individual wooden resonator boxes, felt hammers and small dampers aligned behind the keys, warm maple or birch casework with satin finish, black-and-white piano-style keys, brass and steel action hardware, realistic bench-scale studio product view, clean neutral background, soft directional studio lighting, ~50mm lens, high detail, no labels.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop performance scene of the celesta being softly played, natural light falling across the open service panel so the felt hammers, dampers, steel bars, and resonator boxes are visible, a musician's hands on the keyboard with anatomically plausible posture, small voicing tools and spare felt samples on the bench nearby, quiet prototype-testing atmosphere, realistic wood and metal textures, shallow depth of field, concept visualization only.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the celesta's signature mechanism: one felt hammer rebounding cleanly from a polished steel bar suspended above a rectangular wooden resonator box, a damper lifted just clear of the bar, small pivots and regulation screws visible but not dimensioned, soft raking light across felt, steel, and wood grain, high detail, no measurement marks.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, fake brand marks, strings, piano soundboard, dimension callouts, impossible hammer angles, extra fingers, or hands inside the action while playing.
